Introduction

This SOP details the Bonus Team's responsibilities in managing user bonuses, monitoring for abuse, and ensuring compliance with promotional terms.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Calculate and apply bonuses based on promotions, ensuring compliance with company policies.
  • Monitor for patterns of bonus abuse, such as multi-account usage or suspicious referral codes.
  • Provide assistance to CS agents on bonus-related inquiries.

Step-by-Step Procedure

1. Bonus Calculation and Application

Verify eligibility based on promotion terms and calculate the bonus amount. Ensure accurate application and document all transactions.

2. Detecting Bonus Abuse

Monitor for abusive patterns, such as accounts claiming bonuses excessively or using obscure referral codes. If detected, follow up with Fraud and KYC teams.

3. Responding to CS Inquiries

Assist CS agents with bonus-related inquiries, providing them with up-to-date promotional terms and eligibility criteria.

Escalation Protocols

For suspected abuse, escalate cases to Fraud or KYC via Slack. Request additional documentation, such as ID and selfie with Gambi paper, if necessary.
